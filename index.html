Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 16, 2026

1. Introduction

Scoreboard ("we," "us," or "our") operates the Scoreboard mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our App.

By using Scoreboard, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Account Information

Scoreboard creates your account automatically using a device-linked identifier derived from your device's hardware. No password is required. When you optionally sign in with Apple or Google for display purposes, we may collect:

  • A unique device-derived user identifier (always required)
  • Display name (first name and last initial only shown publicly)
  • Email address (only if provided by your sign-in method; never shown publicly)

2.2 Profile Information

You provide the following during onboarding and profile setup:

  • First and last name
  • Age
  • Gender identity
  • Discovery preferences
  • City or area
  • Bio text
  • Profile photos (3 to 10 images)

2.3 AI Facial Scan

The AI facial scan is processed entirely on your device. We use Apple's Vision framework to measure facial geometry from your profile photos. No photos and no facial geometry data are transmitted to any server. The result is a single numeric score (your AI Rating Score) stored on our servers. The underlying photos and geometry data never leave your device.

2.4 Location Data

Scoreboard requires location access to function. We collect your device's geographic coordinates to:

  • Show you profiles of people in your city and region
  • Calculate your area-specific Scoreboard Score
  • Power location-based leaderboards and Passport stamps
  • Keep your Passport stamps active (geotag refresh)

Your precise coordinates are never shown to other users. Other users see only your general area (e.g., "Hanson" or "Greater Boston").

2.5 Rating and Activity Data

We collect:

  • Ratings you give and receive (scores 1 through 10)
  • Your Scoreboard Score per area
  • Pins balance and transaction history
  • Passport stamp history and timestamps
  • Profiles you have viewed or skipped

2.6 Usage and Device Data

We automatically collect:

  • Device type and operating system version
  • App usage patterns and session data
  • Crash reports and performance metrics via Firebase

2.7 Subscription and Payment Data

If you subscribe to Scoreboard Premium:

  • Subscription status and expiration date (stored by us)
  • Purchase history (processed by Apple, not stored by us)

3. How We Use Your Information

  • Operate and maintain the rating and discovery system
  • Calculate and update your Scoreboard Score and area rankings
  • Match you with compatible profiles in your city and region
  • Manage Passport stamps and chatroom access
  • Award Pins and process in-app economy transactions
  • Enforce community guidelines and prevent abuse
  • Improve the App and develop new features
  • Communicate with you about your account

4. How We Store Your Data

  • Profile data and ratings: Google Firebase Firestore (United States)
  • Profile photos: Google Firebase Storage (United States)
  • Authentication: Google Firebase Authentication
  • Device credential: Stored in your device Keychain only
  • Retention: Until you delete your account

5. Data Sharing

We do NOT sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties, except:

  • Google Firebase (cloud infrastructure and hosting)
  • Google AdMob (advertising displayed to free-tier users only; disabled for Premium subscribers)
  • Apple App Store (payment processing for Premium subscriptions)
  • When required by law, court order, or legal process
  • To protect the safety of our users or the public

6. What Other Users See

Your public profile includes:

  • First name and last initial (e.g., "Steve B.")
  • Age
  • Profile photos
  • Bio
  • General area (not precise location)
  • Your Scoreboard Score (aggregate, not per-rater breakdown)

Other users NEVER see: your full last name, email address, precise GPS coordinates, individual ratings you gave or received, your discovery preferences, or your device identifier.

7. Your Rights

7.1 Access Your Data

Contact us to request a copy of your personal data.

7.2 Delete Your Account

Go to: Profile tab > Settings > Delete Account

This permanently and immediately deletes:

  • Your user profile and all personal information
  • All profile photos from Firebase Storage
  • All ratings you have given
  • Your Scoreboard Scores, Passport stamps, and Pins balance
  • Your Firebase Authentication account
  • All chat history associated with your account
  • This action cannot be undone. There is no recovery process.

7.3 Location Permission

You can revoke location permission at any time in your device Settings. Scoreboard requires location access to function. Revoking permission will prevent you from using discovery, Passport, and Scoreboard features.

8. Data Security

We implement the following security measures:

  • Secure data transmission (HTTPS/TLS)
  • Authentication-required API access
  • Firebase Firestore security rules limiting data access by user
  • Photos stored in authenticated Firebase Storage buckets
  • Device credentials stored exclusively in your device Keychain, never transmitted in plaintext
  • AI facial scan processed on-device only — no biometric data leaves your device

9. Advertising

Free-tier users may see ads served by Google AdMob. Ads are disabled entirely for Premium subscribers. AdMob may use your device's advertising identifier (IDFA) subject to your App Tracking Transparency (ATT) consent. You can limit ad tracking at any time in Settings > Privacy > Tracking.

10. Children's Privacy

Scoreboard is not intended for anyone under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a minor has created an account, contact us immediately.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by updating the "Last Updated" date. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

12. Contact Us

For privacy questions or to exercise your rights:

Email: support@scoreboard.app
